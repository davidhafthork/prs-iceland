Building Iceland's Precision Rifle Community

4 Years Active
150+ Active Members
24 Annual Matches

PRS Iceland has grown from a small group of enthusiasts to Iceland's premier precision rifle organization. We run two competitive series: the centerfire PRS Series and the rimfire PR22 Series, providing opportunities for shooters of all skill levels.

Our matches follow international PRS standards while embracing Iceland's unique shooting culture. From challenging mountain stages to precision work at extended distances, we test every aspect of marksmanship.
